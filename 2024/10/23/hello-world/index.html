Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2024

At RelaxAppsTeam, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our websites, mobile applications, and other online products and services (collectively, the “Services”), or interact with us in any other way.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by revising the date above and, where appropriate, providing additional notice such as a statement on our homepage or an email notification. We encourage you to review this policy whenever you use our Services to stay informed about our practices and your privacy rights.

Collection of Information

Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you voluntarily provide us, such as when you:

  • Create an account.
  • Request customer support.
  • Communicate with us in any other way.

The information we collect may include your email address and any additional information you choose to share.

Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect information, including:

  • Log Information: Details about your usage, such as browser type, access times, pages viewed, and IP address.
  • Device Information: Information about the device you use to access our Services, including device model, operating system, MAC address, and mobile network information.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and web beacons to improve our Services and your experience, and to understand which areas and features are popular.

Information from Other Sources

We may also collect information from third parties and combine it with data collected through our Services.

Use of Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing and improving our Services.
  • Delivering products and services you request.
  • Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support.
  • Sending you updates, security alerts, and administrative messages.
  • Communicating about products, services, and promotional offers.
  • Analyzing and monitoring trends and activities.
  • Personalizing your experience and providing relevant content.
  • Detecting and preventing fraudulent or illegal activities.

Sharing of Information

We may share information about you in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating our Services.
  • In response to legal requests or to protect the rights and safety of RelaxAppsTeam, our users, or others.
  • In the event of a merger, sale, or acquisition of our business.
  • With your consent or at your direction.

We may also share aggregated or de-identified information that does not identify you.

Analytics Services Provided by Others

We may engage third-party analytics providers who use cookies and other technologies to collect data about your online activity. This data helps us understand your preferences and improve our services.

Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access to your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

We do not intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

Your Choices

Device Permissions

You can control access to certain device features, such as your phonebook and image storage, through your device settings.

Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Push Notifications

You can opt-out of receiving push notifications through your device settings.

Contact Us

For any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: relaxappsteam@outlook.com
